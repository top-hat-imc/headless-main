Community Spotlight: Lehigh Acres

Location

Lehigh Acres is located in Lee County, on the Gulf side of Florida. It’s roughly twelve miles east of Fort Myers, and forty miles north of Naples.

Why Lehigh Acres?

As a diverse community of approximately 80,000 residents, the moderately-sized suburban enclave is the ideal home for families or seniors seeking a laid-back living environment. With 175 miles of canals and 16 well-stocked lakes, Lehigh Acres is a prime area for fishing. You can also find four championship golf courses here.

Harn’s Marsh

Explore the beauty of southwest Florida by visiting the abundance of wildlife at Harns Marsh. It’s located in Lee County off of Sunshine Blvd. in Lehigh Acres. Originally, Harns Marsh was a 578-acre farmland. In the 1980’s, it was transformed to an active stormwater facility to help filter water and reduce flooding to the Orange River. The water control structures have helped create a lush habitat for snail kites, limpkin and more than 140 species of animals. Harn’s Marsh is not a park, but a great public exploration destination to hike and observe wildlife.

Five Star Stables

Five Star Stables is your one stop shop for all your Equestrian dreams. Located 13 miles west of Lehigh Acres, Five Star Stables sits on 1800 acres of open land and beautiful trails. Venture out on Trail Rides, Private, Group, and Family Lessons, Barrel Racing, Pole Pending, Birthday Parties and more. 

Golfing

With an average of 267 sunny days per year, golfing is a year round sport in Lehigh Acres. Within a 20 mile radius, there are roughly 52 golf courses. The oldest course in Lehigh Acres, the Cypress Lake Golf Club, opened in 1960. The longest course is Colonial Country Club, which is approximately 7,537 yards. Also, rated one of the best courses near Lehigh Acres, is Fiddlesticks Country Club. As you can see, there are no shortages of golfing opportunities in this small town.

Lehigh Resort Club

Enjoy the outdoors all year long at Lehigh Resort Club. Whether you live nearby and need an escape, or are hosting out of state guests, this 65 acre resort is pure vacation bliss. There are activities to engage guests of all ages: swimming pools, hot tubs, basketball, tennis, and pickleball courts, sand volleyball, bicycle rentals, a day spa, miniature and onsite golf courses, a fitness center, picnic areas and more. Purchase a day pass or stay in one of their lodging accommodations. 


Living in Lehigh Acres offers residents a suburban rural mix feel. With plenty of parks, fishing, and golf courses available, it’s a placid community to raise a family or revel in retirement.
